What ChemoSense Tracks

Six critical vital signs captured continuously, giving oncologists a complete picture of patient health.

ECG / Heart Rate

Continuous single-lead ECG monitoring detects arrhythmias, QT prolongation, and cardiotoxicity markers.

SpO₂ Levels

Blood oxygen saturation tracked continuously to detect respiratory complications early.

98%

Body Temperature

Infrared skin temperature sensor catches febrile episodes — a key sign of neutropenic infections.

36.8°C

Heart Rate Variability

HRV analysis reveals autonomic nervous system stress and neuropathy onset patterns.

Motion & Activity

3-axis accelerometer & gyroscope detect fatigue, tremors, and mobility degradation.

Galvanic Skin Response

GSR sensors detect stress, pain response, and autonomic nervous system activity changes.

How It Works

01

Patient Wears the Device

Comfortable, lightweight wearable designed for 24/7 use throughout the entire treatment cycle.

02

Continuous Data Collection

Six vital parameters captured simultaneously via medical-grade sensors. Real-time streaming via BLE to cloud.

03

AI Analysis & Scoring

Proprietary ML models generate a ChemoSafe Score™ — a real-time risk assessment of treatment complications.

04

Oncologist Dashboard

Real-time alerts, trend analytics, and predictive reports — enabling proactive intervention before complications arise.

AI Engine

Our proprietary AI engine processes thousands of data points per minute to produce a single, actionable risk score.

⚡

Real-Time Inference
